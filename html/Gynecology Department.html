Gynecology Department

The Gynecology Department specializes in women's health, offering personalized care for every stage of life. Services include:

  • Prenatal care and pregnancy follow-ups.
  • Childbirth services with advanced medical equipment.
  • Family planning and fertility consultations.

Our dedicated team ensures the health and well-being of every woman with professionalism and compassion.
