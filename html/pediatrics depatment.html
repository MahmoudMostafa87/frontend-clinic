Pediatrics Department

The Pediatrics Department provides comprehensive healthcare for children, ensuring their healthy growth and development. Services include:

  • Routine check-ups for infants and children.
  • Vaccination programs to prevent common diseases.
  • Treatment for childhood illnesses like flu, infections, and allergies.

Our team of experienced pediatricians is dedicated to providing the best care for your child in a warm and friendly environment.
