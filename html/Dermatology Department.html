Dermatology Department

The Dermatology Department offers expert care for skin, hair, and nail conditions. Services include:

  • Treatment for acne, eczema, and other skin disorders.
  • Advanced skincare treatments for a healthy and radiant appearance.
  • Diagnosis and treatment of hair and scalp problems.

Our dermatologists use modern techniques to provide the best solutions tailored to your needs.
